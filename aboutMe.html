About Me

Hello! I am a graduate from Raffles Institution and a Gold Microsoft Learn Student Ambassador, who plans on studying Computer Science in university. A self-motivated learner, I make the most out of any opportunities I have to have fun whilst pursuing my developing my skills, especially in digital accessibility domain and the Art X Tech intersection. Having studied Spanish for 6 years, I have also developed an interest in languages and their connected histories.

In the future, I hope to make impactful contributions and/or pursue the areas of Digital Accessibility, Assistive Technology, Extended Reality, Computer Graphics and Game Development.

My Journey






Microsoft Learn Student Ambassadors Program (Gold)

As an MLSA, I learn about various Microsoft Technologies and take part in / come up with initiatives to spread the knowledge to the local and global communities.

Notable activities:

Raffles Girls' School (Secondary)

2016 - 2019

RGS was where I got many opportunities to cultivate my interest in STEM, and beyond that, using my ability STEM to help others.
